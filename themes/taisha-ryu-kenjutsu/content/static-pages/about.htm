MULTIMEDIA AT THE SERVICE OF MARTIAL ARTS

Videos - DVD - Posters - Photos - Articles - Books

RONIN Martial Production is an audiovisual creation agency dedicated mainly to martial arts and Japanese culture


based in the Rhône-Alpes region. This production is a "branch" of the CDRAM association. In addition to this, you need to know more about it.


The particularity of this production is that the martial practitioners are in front of and behind the camera.


We therefore put our experience and skills at your service for all types of services: Promotional video, technical DVDs, reports, documentaries but also posters, flyers, logos, etc.


Ronin Martial Production is also a Youtube channel also dedicated to martial arts.


 

PRACTITIONERS IN FRONT AND BEHIND THE CAMERA!


The purpose of this production is to promote traditional martial arts, the Budo tradition 武 道


through its videos and DVDs and your projects


Thanks to this martial production, you will be able to learn, revise, discover the way (道 Do-Michi) of martial arts and Japanese culture


RONIN Martial Production offers you a wide martial horizon that speaks to any practitioner regardless of his discipline!

RMP (RONIN Martial Production) is not aimed at an art, a style, a particular school but at all and that whatever its discipline, its level since even if we do not practice the same art, the same discipline but at the final,


we are walking on the same path ...
